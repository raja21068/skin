1. Clay Masks

Clay Masks

How to Use: DIY Clay Mask

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon bentonite or kaolin clay.
  • 1-2 teaspoons rose water or apple cider vinegar (diluted).

Instructions:

  1. Mix the clay and liquid to form a smooth paste.
  2. Apply evenly to your face, avoiding the eyes and lips.
  3. Leave on for 10-15 minutes or until it dries.
  4. Rinse off with lukewarm water and pat dry.

Frequency:

Use 1-2 times a week for oil control.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera

How to Use: DIY Aloe Gel Moisturizer

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons fresh aloe vera gel.
  • 2-3 drops of tea tree oil (optional, for acne control).

Instructions:

  1. Blend the aloe vera gel until smooth.
  2. Add tea tree oil and mix well.
  3. Store in a clean jar and refrigerate for freshness.
  4. Apply a thin layer after cleansing as a moisturizer.

Frequency:

Daily, morning and evening.

3. Green Tea

Green Tea

How to Use: DIY Green Tea Toner

Ingredients:

  • 1 green tea bag.
  • 1 cup boiling water.

Instructions:

  1. Brew the green tea and let it cool completely.
  2. Pour into a clean spray bottle or jar.
  3. Use a cotton pad to apply to your face after cleansing.

Frequency:

Use twice daily as a toner.

4. Niacinamide

Niacinamide

How to Use: DIY Niacinamide Serum

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon niacinamide powder.
  • 3 tablespoons distilled water.
  • 1/4 teaspoon vegetable glycerin (optional, for hydration).

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve the niacinamide powder in distilled water.
  2. Add glycerin and mix well.
  3. Transfer to a dropper bottle.
  4. Apply a few drops to your face before moisturizing.

Frequency:

Once daily, preferably in the morning.

5. Witch Hazel

Witch Hazel

How to Use: DIY Witch Hazel Toner

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons pure witch hazel.
  • 2 tablespoons rose water.
  • 1-2 drops lavender essential oil (optional, for scent).

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a clean bottle.
  2. Shake well before each use.
  3. Apply with a cotton pad after cleansing.

Frequency:

1-2 times daily.
