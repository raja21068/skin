Natural Skin Care for Combination Skin

Explore a curated guide to balance hydration and control oil with DIY remedies using natural ingredients.

1. Shea Butter

Shea Butter

Applications:

Moisturizer: Warm a small amount between your palms and apply to dry areas like hands, elbows, and face.

Night Treatment: Apply a generous layer before bed for intense hydration.

Frequency:

Use daily or as needed for dryness.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera

Applications:

Soothing Gel: Apply pure aloe vera gel to irritated skin and massage until absorbed.

Face Moisturizer: Use as a lightweight base before applying other products.

Frequency:

Apply multiple times daily, especially after sun exposure.

3. Natural Oils

Jojoba Oil

Applications:

Facial Moisturizer: Apply a few drops to the face and neck after cleansing.

Hair Conditioner: Massage into the scalp and through hair strands to add moisture and shine.

Frequency:

Daily for skin; 2-3 times a week for hair.

Sweet Almond Oil

Applications:

Face Serum: Use a few drops before applying moisturizer.

Body Moisturizer: Massage into dry areas like elbows, knees, and heels.

Frequency:

Use daily, preferably morning and evening.

4. Honey

Honey

Applications:

Face Mask: Apply a thin layer of honey to the face and leave for 15-20 minutes. Rinse with warm water.

Spot Treatment: Dab on dry or irritated patches for quick hydration.

Frequency:

2-3 times per week for best results.

DIY Balanced Moisturizer for Combination Skin

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Shea Butter
  • 1 tbsp Jojoba Oil
  • 1 tsp Aloe Vera Gel
  • 1 tsp Sweet Almond Oil
  • 2-3 drops Lavender Essential Oil

Instructions:

  1. Melt Shea Butter in a double boiler.
  2. Add Jojoba Oil and Sweet Almond Oil; mix well.
  3. Allow to cool slightly, then add Aloe Vera Gel and Lavender Essential Oil.
  4. Whip until creamy and transfer to a sterilized jar.

Application:

Apply a small amount to your face and neck after cleansing. Use morning and evening.
