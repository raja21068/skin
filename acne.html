Acne Care

1. Cleansing

Honey Cleanser

Raw honey has natural antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. Apply a thin layer, massage gently, and rinse off.

Apple Cider Vinegar (Diluted)

Acts as a toner to balance the skin's pH and reduce bacteria. Mix 1 part vinegar with 3 parts water.

Aloe Vera Gel

A gentle cleanser for sensitive and acne-prone skin. Use pure aloe vera directly from the plant or a store-bought natural gel.

2. Exfoliation

Oatmeal Scrub

Mix finely ground oats with water to create a gentle exfoliating paste. It removes dead skin and soothes irritation.

Sugar and Honey Scrub

For occasional use to remove dead skin without damaging the skin barrier.

Baking Soda (Caution)

Use sparingly and in small amounts (mixed with water) as it can irritate sensitive skin.

3. Spot Treatments

Tea Tree Oil

A powerful antibacterial agent. Dilute with a carrier oil (like jojoba or coconut oil) and apply to blemishes.

Turmeric Paste

Mix turmeric powder with water or aloe vera to calm inflammation and redness.

Witch Hazel

A natural astringent that reduces oiliness and tightens pores.

DIY Acne Care Masks

Tea Tree and Honey Mask

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon Tea Tree Oil
  • 2 tablespoons Raw Honey

Instructions:

  1. Mix tea tree oil with raw honey in a small bowl until well combined.
  2. Apply the mixture to clean, dry areas with acne.
  3. Leave it on for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Rinse off with warm water.

Benefits:

Reduces bacteria and soothes inflamed skin, promoting faster healing of blemishes.

Turmeric and Yogurt Mask

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Plain Yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric Powder

Instructions:

  1. Combine plain yogurt with turmeric powder in a bowl.
  2. Mix until you achieve a smooth paste.
  3. Apply the mask to your face, focusing on acne-prone areas.
  4. Leave it on for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Rinse off with lukewarm water.

Benefits:

Calms inflammation, reduces redness, and provides gentle exfoliation to unclog pores.

Witch Hazel and Aloe Vera Mask

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Aloe Vera Gel
  • 1 tablespoon Witch Hazel

Instructions:

  1. Mix aloe vera gel with witch hazel in a bowl until well blended.
  2. Apply the mixture to your face, concentrating on areas with acne.
  3. Leave it on for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Rinse off with cool water.

Benefits:

Reduces oiliness, tightens pores, and provides soothing relief to irritated skin.
